Cyber-Physical Systems and Internet-of-Things Week

San Antonio, Texas USA, May 9-12, 2023

Conference Photos

(Conference photos are credits of Dr. Weining Zhang, Professor Emeritus in Department of Computer Science at UTSA)
CPS-IoT Week is the premier event on Cyber-Physical Systems and the Internet-of-Things. It brings together five top conferences, HSCC, ICCPS, IoTDI, IPSN, and RTAS, multiple workshops, tutorials, and competitions.


CPS-IoT Week 2023 will be held on the Main Campus of The University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA). Considering the current situation, the Organizing Committee has decided to support virtual participation for the main conferences of CPS-IoT Week 2023 while highly encouraging participants to attend the event in-person. Moreover, one author of each accepted paper is required to present the work in-person unless authors obtain pre-approval for remote presentation from the chairs of each conference only for exceptional scenarios.

Harassment

The organizers of the CPS-IoT Week 2023 activities, condemns any harassment activities at its venues. If you believe you have been harassed, you are encouraged to report the incident, in confidence, to the General Co-chairs. Further, please check here for details.
