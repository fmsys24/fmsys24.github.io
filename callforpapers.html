International Workshop on Foundation Models for Cyber-Physical Systems & Internet of Things (FMSys)

Hong Kong, May 13-16, 2024

Call for Papers

Researchers from academia and industry are invited to submit proposals for workshops, tutorials, and competitions to be held as part of FMSys 2024 in Hong Kong on May 13-16, 2024.

Important Dates:

  • Proposal submission deadline: TBD ­­
  • Notification of acceptance: TBD

    • Topics of interest include (but are not limited to)

    1. Foundation models on sensor data analytics, e.g., acoustic, light, motion, RF sensor.
    2. Benchmark datasets/evaluations of foundation models on sensor data.
    3. Applications of foundation models in healthcare, smart city, virtual reality, social media, etc..
    4. Foundation models for reasoning, planning, and control.
    5. Efficient fine-tuning and inference of foundation models for CPS/IoT applications.
    6. Novel machine learning (e.g., federated and multi-modal learning) systems based on foundation models.
    7. Foundation models for human-computer interaction or human-AI collaboration.
    8. Trustworthiness of foundation models.
    Submission requirements: Submissions should be original, unpublished research addressing the above topics. Each submission is a single PDF file no longer than six pages (two-column, 10-point), following ACM conference proceedings format (https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template), including figures, references, etc. Submissions must include author names and affiliations for single-blind peer reviewing by the technical program
